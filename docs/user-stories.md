# User Stories

As an existing fan of comic books and Marvel and DC characters, I want to:
- See data about comic book characters presented in an interesting, engaging and more light-hearted way than other data sources.
- Find out more about my existing favourite characters and see how they are categorised with other characters.
- Find new characters with characteristics that I enjoy and see data about them presented in an entertaining way.

As a new or potential fan of comic books and Marvel and DC characters, I want to:
- Find out more about the categories that different Marvel and DC Comics characters fall into, including their costume colours and superpowers.
- Find my perfect comic book character based on the characteristics that are important to me, including their species, gender and alignment.
- See data about comic book characters presented in an engaging, entertaining and light-hearted way.
