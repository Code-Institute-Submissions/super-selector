Super Selector

Comic book characters are everywhere these days, from movies to breakfast cereals. But if you know nothing about superheroes, how can you feel like you are part of the cultural zeitgeist? Then you might be suffering from:

FOMOOSH*

* Fear Of Missing Out On Superheroes

To cure this dangerous condition just click through the screens and select the characteristics that are most important to you. At the end you will find your perfect comic book character and then you too will be able to impress friends and colleagues with your in-depth knowledge of obscure comic book characters like the great big nerd you truly are!

Let's start simple! First off, let's pick the species that you want your character to be a member of. Just click on the row in the graph below that displays your favourite species name to select it. You can only pick one row at a time, so if you change your mind before continuing then just click on a different row.

When you've made your selection, click on the 'Continue' button to move on to the next selection.

Congratulations! You have selected your character's species and taken the first step into a bigger and more super world. But now let's take things right back to basics: just click on the slice of the pie chart below which contains the name of your preferred character gender, then hit 'Continue'.

And yes, we know there's always more male than female characters. If you want to demand more diversity from your comic books, we're not going to argue...!

Now we're cooking! You have selected the species and gender of your character, so we're getting a better idea of who they are and what they look like. So let's start having a little more fun by clicking on the slice of the pie that represents whether you want your character to be good or evil, benevolent or malevolent, a hero or a villain.

Or a neutral party, we guess. But really, where's the fun in that...?

Whew! That last selection got a little heavy with ethics, but at least we know where your moral compass lies. Let's take things back to the surface level and make a decision based on pure aesthetics. Click on the slice of the pie that contains the colour of costume your ideal character would wear.

If they wear a costume at all, that is!

It's all come down to this.

Your final decision.

The endgame.

Use the selector below to choose your character's superpower. Then hit the 'Finish' button to see who you've ended up with!
