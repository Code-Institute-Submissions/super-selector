Super Selector

Comic book characters are everywhere these days, from movies and video games to action figures, backpacks and even breakfast cereals. But if you know nothing about comic books and superheroes, how can you enjoy a conversation around the water cooler or feel like you are part of the cultural zeitgeist?

Never fear, for the Super Selector is here to cure you of your FOMOOSH (Fear Of Missing Out On Superheroes)!

Simply click through the screens and select the superhuman characteristics that are most important to you. At the end of the Selector, you will find your perfect comic book character and then you too will be able to impress friends and colleagues with your in-depth knowledge of obscure comic book characters like the great big nerd you truly are!

Good luck, and EXCELSIOR!

First up, a pretty straightforward one. Click on the row in the graph below that displays the species you want your character to be a member of. You can only pick one at a time, so if you change your mind before continuing then just click on a different row. When you've made your selection, click on 'Continue' to move on to the next selection.

So you've selected your character's species, and taken the first step into a more super world. Next we'll take it even more simple: just click the slice of the pie chart below which contains the name of your preferred character gender, then hit 'Continue'.

Now we're cooking! Next we have a fun decision: your character's alignment! Click on the slice of the pie that represents whether you want your character to be good or evil, benevolent or malevolent, a hero or a villain.

That last selection got a little heavy with ethics and morality. Let's take things back to the surface level and make a decision based on pure aesthetics. Click on the slice of the pie that contains the colour of costume your ideal character would wear. If they wear a costume at all, that is!

Final decision time. Use the list below to select your character's superpower, then hit the 'Finish' button to see who you've ended up with!
